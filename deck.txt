### **Validating Durability & Sustainability with Real-World Data**

Lab tests and short-term feedback are valuable, but they cannot fully replicate the cumulative stress of a long-distance expedition. This creates a gap between controlled testing and how products truly perform and endure over their lifetime.

This project closes that gap. A **five-month, 3,000 km thru-hike** provides an unparalleled R&D environment to collect objective, long-term data on material degradation, validating the core principles of durability and circularity your brand is built on.

**(Slide 4: The Methodology)**

### **From Subjective Feedback to Quantitative Analysis**

This project moves beyond opinion to provide objective, actionable data.

  * **Systematic Data Capture:** Standardized daily logs and high-resolution photography will be used to track wear patterns at key stress points (e.g., shoulder straps, seams, high-abrasion zones).
  * **Quantitative Metrics:** The analysis will focus on objective performance indicators, including DWR degradation, insulation loft loss, and fabric durability.
  * **Circularity & Durability Assessment:** The methodology is designed to identify specific wear patterns and failure points, providing direct insights to inform design for longevity and repairability.
